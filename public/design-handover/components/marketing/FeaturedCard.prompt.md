Marketing hero card — a photo under a forest scrim with a CTA; use on the marketing surface (light theme).

```jsx
<FeaturedCard
  image="../../assets/imagery/peak-misty.png"
  eyebrow="Performance"
  title="Tren på det du trenger"
  description="Data-drevet coaching, PlayerHQ og TrackMan i én plattform."
  ctaLabel="Book gratis kartlegging"
/>
```

- Forest scrim over the photo; eyebrow is lime, title white. Without `image`, a forest surface is used.
- **ctaVariant**: any Button variant (default `primary`). **align**: `bottom` (default) | `center`.
- This is the loud marketing moment — forest + photo. Inside the app, prefer plain `Card`.
