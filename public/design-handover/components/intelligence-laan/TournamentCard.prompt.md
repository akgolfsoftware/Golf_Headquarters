Tournament list/discovery/calendar card.

```jsx
<TournamentCard series="Norgescup 2026 · RD 1 av 4" name="Hvam Golfklubb" date="15. jun 2026"
  course="Hvam Golfklubb" fieldSize={142} par={72} top25Cut="68,8" active onOpen={() => {}} />
```

`variant="compact"` drops the stat row for dense list views; `active` adds the pulsing live dot for an in-progress event.
