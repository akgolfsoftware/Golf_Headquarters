Mono-caps mini-label that sits above a number or marks a section; the system's signature micro-typography.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Stall-SG · 30 dager</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="signal">Oppdatert for 3 min</Eyebrow>
```

- **tone**: `muted` (default), `default` (brighter grey), `signal` (lime).
- Always UPPERCASE, JetBrains Mono 600, tracking 0.12em. Keep labels short.
